H2 Green Valley Demonstrator — Questionnaire

User Experience Study

Purpose of the Questionnaire
Please read this information before proceeding.

The purpose of this questionnaire is to assess the user experience associated with the operation of a hydrogen-powered domestic water heater installed in the H2 Green Valley Demonstrator at the HyLab Safety Laboratory.

The questionnaire aims to collect information regarding:

  • User characteristics and previous experience with domestic water heating technologies.
  • Performance of the hydrogen-powered water heater, including hot water availability, temperature control, temperature stability and overall user satisfaction.
  • User confidence, comfort and acceptance regarding the use of hydrogen-powered domestic appliances.
  • Suggestions and feedback that may contribute to the future development and optimisation of hydrogen-based domestic energy technologies.
The information collected will be used for research, development and demonstration activities related to hydrogen technologies. Results may be analysed in an aggregated and anonymised form for technical reports, scientific publications and dissemination activities.

Participation is voluntary and all personal information will be treated confidentially. Personal information will be converted into anonymised participant identifiers prior to data analysis.

Participants may take part in multiple test sessions throughout the study. In such cases, a separate questionnaire shall be completed after each test session. The responses provided should reflect only the participant’s experience during the specific test session.

Estimated completion time: approximately  5–10 minutes.
